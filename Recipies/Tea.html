One Cup of Tea

This will guide you through making a simple cup of tea.

DO NOT USE A MICROWAVE

You will need:

  • Kettle
  • A decent tea bag
  • Milk (Optional)
  • Spoon
  • Sugar (Optional)
  • Favourite Mug

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in kettle
  2. Put tea bag into your mug and pour the boiled water. Stir (steep) until dark
  3. Remove tea bag and put in the bin.
  4. Allow to cool for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add a splash of milk.
  6. Add sugar to your tastes.
