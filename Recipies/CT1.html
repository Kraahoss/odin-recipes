Cheese on Toast for Noobs

This recipie will take approximately 5-10 minutes of your time.

Ingredients:

  • Grated cheese of your choice.
  • Two slices of bread
  • Worchestershire Sauce
  • (Optional: Onions)

Equipment:

  • Oven
  • Sharp knife
  • Bandages

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven on the grill setting to a medium to high heat
  2. Place two slices of bread on the grill and cook until golden brown
  3. Flip over and add cheese (And onions) with a splash of Worchestershire Sauce
  4. Cook until the cheese is bubbling and golden.
